
# FloMDP Client User Guide

Welcome to FloMDP!

This guide will help you use the FloMDP app and understand its main features. No technical knowledge is required.

## What is FloMDP?
FloMDP is an app that helps you manage orders, production, inventory, accounts, and more, all in one place.

## Getting Started
- **Open the App:** Tap the FloMDP icon on your device.
- **Login:** Enter your username and password. If you are new, use the "Sign Up" option if available.

## Main Features

### 1. Dashboard
- See an overview of your activities, tasks, and important updates.

### 2. Orders
- View, create, and manage orders.
- Check order status and details.

### 3. Inventory
- View current inventory levels.
- Track items in stock and their status.

### 4. Production
- Access the production dashboard for ongoing and upcoming jobs.
- View the production queue and dispatch information.
- Report and track production losses.

### 5. Accounts
- View your account dashboard.
- Track purchases, order history, and dispatches.

### 6. Calendar
- See important dates, deadlines, and scheduled events.

### 7. Reports
- Access various reports for insights and summaries.

### 8. Lab Testing
- View lab test results and related information (if enabled for your account).

### 9. Notifications
- Get reminders and alerts for important actions and deadlines.

### 10. Settings
- Change your password, notification preferences, and other personal settings from the menu (gear icon).

### 11. Logging Out
- Use the menu to log out safely when you are done.

## Tips
- Explore each section using the menu or dashboard.
- Tap on items to view more details or take action.
- Use notifications to stay updated.

## Need Help?
- Look for a "Help" or "Support" option in the app menu.
- Contact your organization or the support team if you need assistance.
